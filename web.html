ABOUT US
We are a HealthTech organization promoting equitable health access in Tanzania through building and providing digital healthcare services.
Founded in Tanzania since 2015, we serve marginalized Tanzanians identified through poverty, limited awareness, and negative cultural perceptions affecting their well-being. These are youths, those in the informal sector, rural poor,
and indigenous communities.
